About Us

Benon Associates Limited was started in 1981 and has given birth to subsidiary companies like Benon Interiors as a 100% distribution oriented unit specializing in doormats and rugs. Benon has a dynamic team of professionals in the flooring industry engaged in connecting flooring products to the African Market.

We represent more than twenty reputed floor covering industries from all over the world who manufacture a wide range of floor coverings with different materials and constructions. Our operation team has many years of experience dealing with and providing support from large organizations. Our strength lies in the strong understanding of customer requirements and identifying the right manufacturers to meet those exact standards.

Benon has been certified as a member of Aba chamber of commerce and industries and our social accountability standard guarantees a work environment that meets or exceeds local as well as international legal requirements. Our head office is situated at No. 103 Okigwe Road Aba, Abia state with branch offices in different parts of the country.



Mission Statement

We promise to provide world class floor coverings to our esteemed customers using the expertise of our highly trained professionals with a view to prospering the returns on investments while at the same time respecting the regulatory agencies, host communities and environment.



Vision Statement

We invite you to associate with a highly experienced team with a strong vision and dedication to be the most progressive floor covering value providers in Africa.



Quality Control System

Benon has implemented a comprehensive quality control system which conforms to the requirements of Standard Organization Of Nigeria assessment program.
